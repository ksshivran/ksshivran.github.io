About

I am a PhD researcher at IISc Bengaluru working on cyber-physical security of electric power grids, advised by Prof. Sarasij Das. My research spans substation topology modelling (IEC 61850 / node-breaker model), Bayesian attack graphs, vulnerability assessment, and cyber-physical attack impact analysis.

I am seeking a researcher / postdoctoral / developer role where I can apply my expertise in power system resilience, communication protocols (IEC 61850, GOOSE, SV, MMS), and cybersecurity.
